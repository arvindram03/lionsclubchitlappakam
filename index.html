What we desire?

Serve with joy and be an helping hand in uplifting our society.

  • Enlight growing children through social awarness, financial support, various competitions and awards.
  • Clean sanitary facilities in Govt. Schools and public places.
  • Pure drinking water through RO plants in homes for challenged children.
  • Financial aids to orphanages and celebrating festivals with them as a family.

How we do?

Transforming our desires to action would be impossible without the full hearted efforts of the members amidst their busy schedule. Our donators were also very generous to fund us for all our activities to serve the needed

Our donors are Chitlapakkam Charitable Trust, Ella Rajamanickam Charitable Trust, MJF Lion S. Sivakumar, MJF Lion K. Raghuraj and many more. We would like to thank all of them for their contributions.
